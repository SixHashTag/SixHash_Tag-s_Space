✨ The Universe ✨

A vast open space,
expanding every minute.
A place that holds time,
and more planets we could visit.

A place with many galaxies,
and stars filled in the night sky.
Oh how I wish I could go out there,
and fly so high.

The universe is so beautiful,
so dark and mysterious.
As the stars shimmer and glow in the night,
like a garden filled with roses.

We are lucky as can be,
to have a place like this.
Our universe is our home,
and we thank the Gods for this gift.
